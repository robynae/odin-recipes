Mac and Cheese Recipe

This creamy baked mac and cheese is a perfect crowd pleaser.

Whenever I am asked to bring a dish to any event, this is my go to. I have yet to find someone who dislikes this mac and cheese. Perfectly gooey and cheesy, this mac is sure to have people begging for the recipe!

Ingredients

For the noodles:

  • 1 lb elbow macaroni
  • 16 oz chicken stock
  • 1-2 tbsp Better Than Bouillon chicken base
  • 8 tbsps high quality butter- Kerrygold works great!

For the cheese sauce:

  • 1 lb shredded cheddar cheese- keep about half separated
  • 1/2 lb shredded gouda cheese
  • 1/2 lb shredded gruyere cheese
  • 8 tbsps good quality butter
  • 8 tbsps all-purpose flour
  • About 3 cups of milk
  • About 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 8oz block of cream cheese
  • Lawry's seasoning salt
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Paprika
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Frank's Redhot
  • 1 tsp Sweetened Condensed Milk

Cooking instructions:

A quick note: make sure you are using cheese that you grate yourself - pre-shredded cheese has a coating on it that prevents proper melting.

Alright, let's get into it! For the noodles:

  1. Bring chicken stock to a boil, flavor it further with Better than Bouillon
  2. Boil mac and cheese until tender, you still want some bite to it, as it will be baked later
  3. Drain the mac and cheese once cooked
  4. Melt a stick of butter into the noodles
  5. Set aside until ready to assemnle

For the cheese sauce:

  1. Melt your butter in a large, oven=proof pot. I prefer an enameled dutch oven
  2. Once butter is melted, whisk in your flour. Whisk constantly until the flour has incorporated, and for one additional minute to get rid of raw glour taste
  3. Slowly pour in your milk and heavy whipping cream, stirring constantly until everything is mixed together
  4. Add in your spices and hot sauce- salt can wait until the end but I still always add some in this step
  5. Let sit on medium heat, stirring frequently until the mixture evenly coats the back of a spoon. Then remove off heat
  6. Add in cream cheese, stur until melted
  7. Add in the rest of your cheese, stir until creamy and melted
  8. Add in your teaspoon or 2 of sweetened condensed milk- trust me
  9. Stir in your cooked noodles

Baking instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Cover your mac and cheese with the cheddar cheese you set aside earlier
  3. Once oven is preheated, bake mac and cheese for about 30 minutes, until the cheese turns brown and bubbly
  4. Enjoy!
