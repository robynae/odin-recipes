Butternut Squash Risotto Recipe

This risotto is perfect for chilly fall evenings.

As soon as the leaves begin to turn color, I rush to the grocery store to pick up the ingredients for this fall recipe. This rich risotto will be your new go-to!

Ingredients

For the risotto:

  • 1 cup arborio rice
  • 32 oz chicken stock
  • 1 large onion, finely diced
  • 1-2 cups dry white wine
  • 1 8oz block parmesan cheese, grated

For the butternut puree:

  • 1 butternut squash, halved
  • Sage leaves
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

For the garnishments:

  • 6 tbsps butter
  • Sage leaves
  • Salt
  • Bacon
